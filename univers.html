Description

Stellaria is quite a cold person and not very expressive. She is very lonely.
Ever since she was little, she has been very intelligent and precocious. She devoured tons of books from her father's library. She is passionate about everything related to human vision, the eye, light and colors.
She is a very unsociable person, she does not necessarily like to talk and likes to spend time alone.
She does not possess magic but uses firearms and visual traps, illusions to fight her enemies.
