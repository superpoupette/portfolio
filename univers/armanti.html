Armanti is a fairly sensitive person who doesn't show it. She seems very sure of herself and decided at first. She is very determined and stubborn.
She is a very religious person, faith has a big place in her life. She also likes to take care of others, and is a very dedicated person.
She possesses fairly weak earth-type magic. Barely able to crack the ground, but that allows her to find his way in space. The seed in his scepter serves as an amplifier of power. Thanks to it, she is now able to create earthquakes.

Childhood

27 december 1254

Armanti grew up in Vehmas. The whole community of Vehmas is a family. Blind from birth, she was put aside at a very young age by the other children who refused to play with her. She spent a lot of time alone in nature.

Maeve's Reign

The cult of the Father Tree is deeply rooted in Vehmas, but Armanti's faith grew stronger. She found a comforting and calming presence in nature and in the Tree. She was very involved in the religious community and became a high priestess.

Destruction of Vehmas

When the forest was burnt, Armanti saw her people die and her home destroyed. Her pain was immense.
She is the last of her species. She would have liked to die with them, but she accepted the mission entrusted to her: to bring the forest back to life. She carries in her amber scepter a seed that can revive the Father Tree and searches for a place to put it there.
