Maeve is a very emotionally fragile young girl. She is extremely sensitive and feels indebted to the people of Vehmas who gave her a loving home. She is very naive and easily manipulated.

She is quite introverted, she likes nature and walks alone in the forest. She really enjoys reading novels that some forest people give her in secret. They allow her to escape. She has a lot of imagination and is very dreamy.
She has no magic, but she has learned to use that of the magical beings around her. She is a very good herbalist.

Childhood

13/07/1255 or 13/07/1987

Maeve was born in Marseille, France in another universe. She grew up in a large family of 7 children. One day she fell into a rift that led her to Vehmas, where she met the fairy people who considered her their chosen one and raised her as a princess.

Reign

At the age of 15, Maeve was crowned Queen of Vehmas. For years she will fulfill this role but will gradually turn into a tree like all the kings who came before her. As a queen, her powers are very limited. She is content to be present at public events but does not make any important decisions.
Some time after her coronation, she is married to a Cyané, Kaoda, to ease the tensions of her people.

Destruction of Vehmas

Maeve gradually realizes that her role is not to rule the forest but to sacrifice herself for it. Her body, which turns into a plant, will have to join the Father tree to nourish it with its essence. Although her relations with Kaoda are only friendly, the one who suffers the people and who sees his wife fail, goes mad. He decides to destroy the Father tree to free those he loves. The destruction of the tree results in the death of the entire forest and breaks down a natural barrier that prevented monsters from reaching human territory.
